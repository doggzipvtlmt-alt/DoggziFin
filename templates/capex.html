What is CAPEX?

Capital Expenditure refers to funds spent on acquiring, upgrading, or maintaining long-term physical or intangible assets for Doggzi's cloud kitchen operations.

  • Cloud kitchen setup
  • Cooking equipment
  • Refrigeration units
  • Technology infrastructure
  • App development
  • Expansion investments
